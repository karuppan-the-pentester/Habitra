Features · 01 / 04

Everything you need.
Nothing more.

Habitra is six ideas, cleanly implemented. No streak guilt, no gamification theater — just the loop, working.

Swipe to complete

Every tap is a microcommit

Swipe a habit left to complete, right to skip with a reason. No modals, no friction. Your week lights up as you go.

Time of day

Morning, afternoon, evening

Habits are grouped by time. The current period is highlighted — you see exactly what's next.

Habit stacks

One cue, many habits

Stack habits so each triggers the next. After coffee → meditate → journal. The chain is the cue.

Streak freezes

Streaks that forgive

Miss a day? Your streak freezes, not breaks. Earn freezes by being consistent.

Guided routines

Press play, follow steps

Turn any habit into a timed routine with named steps. Habitra walks you through it.

Offline first

Yours, even without Wi-Fi

Everything works offline. Logs sync when you're back. Your data stays on-device.

Deep dive · The week

Seven dots tell the whole story.

Each habit shows its last seven days at a glance. No charts. No spreadsheets. A single rhythm, visible.

The dots ripple-fill as you complete — a quiet visual reward that doesn't beg for attention.

Deep dive · Stacks

Chain habits to existing cues.

BJ Fogg-style habit stacking, built in. Anchor a new habit to one you already do — and Habitra reminds you when the prerequisite hasn't happened yet.

The trigger isn't a notification. It's the habit you just finished.
